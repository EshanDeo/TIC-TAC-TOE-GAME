WELCOME TO TIC TAC TOE GAME!

HOW TO PLAY:

The Board: Draw a simple grid of nine squares (3 rows and 3 columns).

Players: Two players take turns. One player uses "X" as their mark, and the other uses "O".

Turns: Players alternate placing their mark in an empty square on the grid.

Winning: The first player to get three of their marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins the game.

Tie: If all nine squares are filled and no player has three in a row, the game ends in a tie.
